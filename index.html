About JBKF

Overview: The JBKF or Jingle Bells Kick Face is a story composed of various series based on the jingle bells and its combination with faces being kicked. This is a fictional and casual story with exceptional chapters which is not like any other piece of literature.

Characters: Santa Claus, Monica, Ben, Charlie, Paraoh, Diamonde.

Temporary characters: Given above are the main characters of JBKF, all other charecters mentioned in the story are temporary and have less significance.

Additional Info

Contributors: The primary contributor is Parin and secondary contributor is Aaroh, yet others too are allowed to write abiding the rules.

History: JBKF was originally invented on 8 and 9 December 2022 by Parin. But was officially created on 20 August 2023. V1 in the 'Volumes' series is the main JBKF

Paradox: In JBKF, there is one single paradox that revolves around every series i.e. "Santa kicks face because it written in the poem" and "It's written in the poem because Santa kicks face". Whereas Monica stands in between to fill the gap.
