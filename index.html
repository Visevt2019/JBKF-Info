About JBKF

Overview: The JBKF or Jingle Bells Kick Face is a casual, fictional meta‑series built around humor and absurdity. It spans various interconnected arcs, each with its own theme and style, but all united by a playful spirit. JBKF is interesting, light, and deliberately chaotic — a universe where paradoxes, satire, and the act of kicking faces become part of the lore. It’s not one genre, but many: a collection of series that together form a humorous, meta‑narrative experiment designed to entertain and surprise.

Characters: Santa Claus, Monica, Ben, Charlie, Paraoh, Diamonde.

Temporary characters: Given above are the main characters of JBKF, all other charecters mentioned in the story are temporary and have less significance.
